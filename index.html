My study experience
& my design works

Study in such a fantasitic major is exciting and challenging.

For my own experience, the major of Media Design is a complex major that includes multiple subjects, such as 3D modeling, Animation, Design as Inquiry, Creative Coding and Photography. These subject seems to be independent, but during my learning progress, they became interconnected and could help each other.

In my design works, inevitably, I combined my elements from my eastern culture background with the new knowledges I learned in western countries. The results of these combinations sometimes could bring me to a new aesthetic level.

Meanwhile, I am trying to cultivate my personal aesthetics as well.
Evertime I finish my work, I am happy to summarize my design process and find the drawbacks or some points I could do better in the future.
I believe that improving myself all the time is the best way to achieve succeed.
